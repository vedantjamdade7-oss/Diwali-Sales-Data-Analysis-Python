# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis (Python Project)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **Diwali sales data** using Python to uncover customer purchasing behavior and business insights.

The goal is to simulate real-world data analysis tasks such as customer segmentation, sales trends, and product performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Analyze purchasing behavior during festive season  
- Discover top-performing products and categories  
- Understand demographic trends (age, gender, occupation)  
- Support data-driven marketing strategies  

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

- 👤 Customer details (Gender, Age, Occupation, Marital Status)  
- 🏙️ Location data (State, Zone)  
- 🛍️ Product details (Category, Product ID)  
- 💰 Sales data (Amount, Orders)  

---

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 👩 Gender Analysis
- Female customers contributed more to total sales  
- Higher purchasing power observed among women  

### 🎂 Age Group Analysis
- Age group **26–35** showed highest spending  
- Young adults are key target customers  

### 💼 Occupation Analysis
- IT, Healthcare, and Aviation professionals spent more  
- Working professionals drive major revenue  

### 🏙️ State-wise Analysis
- Top states: **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka**  
- High sales concentration in metro and tier-1 cities  

### 💍 Marital Status
- Married individuals contributed more to sales  
- Family-based purchasing trends observed  

### 🛍️ Product Category Analysis
- Top categories:
  - Food  
  - Clothing & Apparel  
  - Electronics  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

✔ Female customers dominate purchase volume  
✔ Age group 26–35 is the most valuable segment  
✔ Married customers spend more during festivals  
✔ Certain states drive majority of revenue  
✔ Specific product categories outperform others
